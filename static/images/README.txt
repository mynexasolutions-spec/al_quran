IMAGES FOLDER
=============
Place the following files in this directory:

1. logo.png         — Your institute logo (square, PNG with transparent bg recommended)
2. hero-bg.jpg      — Hero background image (landscape, 1600x900px or larger recommended)
                      You can use any mosque/Quran-themed photo.
                      Free options: https://unsplash.com (search "quran" or "mosque")

The site will load fine without hero-bg.jpg (it will use a solid teal gradient).
The logo will show as broken image until logo.png is placed here.
