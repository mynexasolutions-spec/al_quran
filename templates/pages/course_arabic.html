Embark on a foundational journey to speak basic Arabic with our Three-Month Online Basic Arabic Speaking Course. Tailored for beginners of all ages, this intensive program emphasizes conversational skills, everyday vocabulary, and simple grammar, with a focus on Quranic terms and Islamic contexts to enhance your spiritual learning. Delivered through engaging online sessions, you'll gain confidence in speaking Arabic quickly and effectively.

What You'll Learn

  • Basic Pronunciation: Master the Arabic alphabet, sounds, and simple words from scratch
  • Conversational Basics: Greetings, self-introductions, asking questions, and describing daily routines
  • Everyday Vocabulary: Family, shopping, directions, time, and key Quranic terms such as salat and dua
  • Simple Grammar: Sentence structure, verbs, and adjectives for practical everyday use
  • Cultural Insights: Arabic customs and Islamic phrases used in English-speaking Muslim communities
  • Quranic Arabic: An introduction to common words and expressions found throughout the Quran

Why Learn Arabic?

Arabic is the language of the Quran — learning it opens the door to understanding the divine message directly, without relying solely on translations. Even a basic foundation in Arabic will transform your experience of Salah, your reading of the Quran, and your connection to Islamic scholarship.

This course is the ideal first step for anyone who wants to connect more deeply with Islamic knowledge and the global Muslim community.

Requirements & Certification

  • No prior Arabic knowledge required — open to absolute beginners of all ages
  • Stable internet connection, a device with a microphone, and a notebook for practice
  • Maintain at least 70% attendance throughout the three-month program
  • Pass a final speaking exam demonstrating basic conversational Arabic proficiency
