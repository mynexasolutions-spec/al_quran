"It will be said to the companion of the Quran: Recite and rise in rank!"

— Sunan Abi Dawud

Master the art of Quranic recitation with our Online Quran Qirat Course at Al-Qur'an Global Institute. Designed for learners with basic recitation knowledge, this program focuses on the most renowned Qira'at (recitation styles), helping you recite the Quran with precision, proper Tajweed, and a deep spiritual connection. Explore the rich diversity of Quranic recitation and work toward earning your own Ijazah certification.

What You'll Learn

  • Advanced Tajweed: Ikhfa, Idgham, Madd, Qalqalah, and all related rules for precise recitation
  • Makharij and Sifaat: articulation points and letter characteristics for flawless pronunciation
  • Waqf and Ibtida: correct pausing and starting rules to fully preserve Quranic meaning
  • The 10 authentic Qira'at styles including their specific rules, traditions, and applications
  • Quranic Insights: basic Tafsir of selected passages to connect with the Quran's teachings
  • Preparation for the Ijazah pathway under certified scholars with chain of narration

  • Basic Quran recitation knowledge is recommended; beginners should complete a foundational course first

  • Maintain at least 70% attendance throughout the program
  • Pass the comprehensive Qirat Exam to receive certification
  • Advanced students may pursue the full Ijazah pathway
