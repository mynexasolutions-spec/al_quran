Discover the transformative journey of memorizing the Holy Quran with our Quran Hifz Course at Al-Qur'an Global Institute.   • Connect deeply with the Quran through understanding of what you memorize

What You'll Learn

  • Complete memorization of the Quran using the structured sabaq, sabaqi & manzil revision system
  • Effective memorization techniques for long-term retention using spaced repetition

  • Confidence and fluency to recite and share the Quran with others
