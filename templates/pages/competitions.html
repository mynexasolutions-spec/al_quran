Simple Steps

How to Participate

Joining our competitions is straightforward. Follow these steps to register and compete with learners worldwide.

01

Choose Your Competition

Browse the competitions above and find one that matches your skill — Qirat, Quiz, Tajweed, Hifz, Calligraphy, or Essay.

02

Click “Register Now”

Each competition card has a dedicated registration form. Fill in your details and submit — you’ll receive a confirmation message instantly, insha’Allah.

03

Prepare & Perform

Follow the provided rubric to prepare. All competitions are conducted online via video or submission — participate from anywhere in the world.

04

Receive Your Award

Winners and outstanding participants receive digital certificates, prizes, and recognition on our platform and social media channels.
