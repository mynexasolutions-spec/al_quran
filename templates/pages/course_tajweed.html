"And recite the Qur'an distinctly in a measured tone."

— Al-Muzzammil: 4

Reciting the Qur'an is more than reading words — it is an act of worship, a way to draw closer to Allah جل جلاله, and a chance to reflect on His message with the respect it deserves. Our Tajweed Course is designed to help you recite the Qur'an beautifully and accurately, just as it was revealed. Whether you are a beginner or looking to refine your skills, we will guide you with care and clarity. Every letter you recite will become a source of peace for your heart and reward in your Hereafter.

What You'll Learn

  • Basic rules of Tajweed explained simply and clearly for easy understanding at every level
  • Makharij — the origin and articulation points of each Arabic letter for perfect pronunciation
  • Sifaat — the characteristics of letters that affect how they are pronounced
  • Rules of Noon Saakin, Tanween, Meem Saakin, and their different applications
  • Madd (elongation) rules — all types covered with practical recitation examples
  • Rules of Waqf (stopping) and Ibtida (starting) to preserve the meaning of verses
  • How to avoid the most common mistakes made in Qur'an recitation
  • Memorisation of the last 20 Surahs with proper Tajweed application
  • Daily practice with personalised correction and feedback from experienced instructors

Why This Course?

Through this course, you will not only improve your recitation but also develop a deeper emotional and spiritual connection with the Qur'an. Every rule learned brings you closer to reciting the words of Allah exactly as they were revealed — a tremendous honour and blessing.

Begin your journey today — beautify your recitation, strengthen your faith, and let the Qur'an be your lifelong companion. 📚

Certification Requirements

  • Demonstrate correct application of Tajweed rules in live recitation
  • Pass a final Tajweed proficiency assessment set by the instructor
